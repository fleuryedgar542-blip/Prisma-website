Why ordinary planners often fail

Standard systems start with tasks, goals and discipline. Neurodivergent reality often starts somewhere else: low energy, a busy nervous system, changing focus, or a day that already feels full before it begins.

What PRISMA does instead

PRISMA starts with energy, needs and context. That creates calmer choices, smaller next steps and more realistic plans. The goal is not perfect productivity. The goal is usable structure.

Plan with your energy, not against it.
