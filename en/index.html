Recognition

A hard day may look something like this.

You often do know what needs doing, but:

  • you still do not start
  • everything feels slightly too much
  • ordinary planners mostly help on better days

Your head is already full.

Your energy is not steady.

You need something smaller, not stricter.

Pushing harder usually does not help then. Regaining a little overview does.

Why PRISMA helps

PRISMA does not start with performance.

It first looks at what your system can carry today. Then it helps you see what feels possible:

  • less noise
  • more overview
  • a first step that actually fits

First some grip. Then a plan.

The tools
