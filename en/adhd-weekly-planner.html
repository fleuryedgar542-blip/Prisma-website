ADHD weekly planner

A weekly planner for ADHD without extra pressure.

Weekly planning can feel too big and too vague at the same time. You want overview, but the moment a week fills up with blocks, tasks and expectations, it can feel exhausting before it has even started.

Why ordinary weekly planners often fail

Most weekly planners are built for predictability. They assume you already know how much focus you will have on Thursday, how much recovery you will need on Friday and what Saturday will feel like. With ADHD, that is often unrealistic.

What actually helps

  • Overview that shows patterns without becoming strict
  • Energy awareness instead of task obsession
  • Room to adjust the week when life changes
  • Gentle insight instead of more self-criticism

How PRISMA Week works

PRISMA Week lets you track seven days of energy and needs. From there, it gives you a weekly and monthly view that helps you notice what repeats. That makes planning less abstract. You start building from your own patterns, not from a perfect version of yourself.

Plan from patterns, not pressure.

PRISMA Week is designed for calmer weekly planning, especially when energy shifts and ordinary systems get too rigid too quickly.
