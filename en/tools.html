Tools

Three calm tools for moments when things do not feel steady.

One for today. One for a week that feels off balance. And one for the moment you can feel your system filling up faster than you can explain it. PRISMA Day and PRISMA Signal are free. PRISMA Week is paid.

Free daily check-in

PRISMA Day

A calm ADHD check-in for days when you do not know where to begin. It starts with how you are doing, not with tasks, and helps you see what feels manageable today.

  • Useful when your day feels foggy, heavy or overstimulating
  • Short enough to use even on low-capacity mornings
  • Designed to lower shame instead of adding pressure

Paid weekly overview

PRISMA Week

A calm weekly overview for weeks that keep slipping out of your hands. It helps you notice patterns in energy and needs, so the next week can feel a little clearer and more manageable.

  • Helpful when an entire week starts to feel too big
  • Shows how needs and energy shift across several days
  • Turns reflection into calmer planning instead of self-criticism

Sensory check-in

PRISMA Signal

A quick sensory check-in that helps you notice overload early. It is especially useful when something feels off or too much, but you cannot yet tell what would help.

  • Fast enough to use in the middle of the day
  • Helps you put words to what your system is carrying
  • Pairs well with autism-friendly planning and recovery routines
