Step 1, How much energy do you have right now?
1
Almost empty
Your body and mind need rest. Everything feels heavy.
2
Low
You can do something, but not much. Light tasks are realistic.
3
Middle
Fairly steady. You can function, but pushing will not help.
4
Good
You have some room. Use it on purpose, not all at once.
5
Full
You feel good. This is a good day for something that asks a bit more.
Step 2, What does your body or mind need?
🛋 I want a bit of breathing room
🔇 I want less input
🚶 I want to move or go outside
📋 I want to know what is coming
💬 I want contact with someone
🎨 I want to make or think up something
📱 I want something in the background
🎧 I want music or sound with me
⏱ I want to know when it ends
🙈 I do not want to be interrupted

You can choose more than one. Leaving it empty is fine too.

Step 3, How do you work best?
More than one thing at once
Something in the background helps your brain stay engaged.
One thing at a time
You would rather finish one thing first. Loose ends feel noisy.
Blocks with breaks
Work, stop, recharge, then continue. Clear blocks help.
I follow the day as it goes
No fixed approach needed. You notice what works now and go with that.

If you are not sure yet, leaving it empty is fine.

Step 4, Is there something you truly have to do today?

Something that cannot wait, even if today feels tight.

Step 5, Who do you want to be today?

One word or a short sentence is enough. You can leave it empty too.
