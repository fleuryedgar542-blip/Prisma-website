About me

I am not building this from a distance. I am in the middle of it too.

I am Fleur, founder of PRISMA Systemen. I have ADHD and autism, and I know what it is like to want a lot from life while repeatedly running into systems that simply were not made for your brain.

I do not have all the answers. I am not writing from a place where everything is already solved.

What I do have is honesty about what this really feels like, and the tools I kept wishing existed. PRISMA comes from lived experience. Not as a polished success story, but as something that is still moving.

Why PRISMA

What makes PRISMA different.

  • No pep talk, just clarity

    PRISMA is not here to hype you up. I would rather make something that feels true and useful, including on days when everything is slower and harder.

  • From the inside

    I am not looking at this from the outside. I live with it. That shapes how I write, what I leave out and what I decide matters.

  • Independence first

    The goal is not to make you dependent on me or on a system. The goal is to give you something you can genuinely use.

  • Calm over noise

    No busy visuals, no loud copy and no sense of urgency for the sake of it. Just what helps.

What else is going on

PRISMA alongside real life.

In September 2026 I am starting a Social Work degree. I am building PRISMA in the space I have now, and after that I will shape it around what remains realistic alongside studying.

That means no big promises about speed or availability. Just honesty about what I can and cannot offer.
