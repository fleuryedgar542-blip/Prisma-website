About

PRISMA was built for calmer planning.

PRISMA Systems was created by Fleur Yedgar from a simple observation: many neurodivergent people are not failing at planning, they are being handed planning systems that were never designed for the way their brains and nervous systems actually work.

What PRISMA stands for

PRISMA is not about squeezing more productivity out of people. It is about making planning gentler, clearer and more honest. The tools are intentionally calm, lightweight and local-first, so the experience itself does not become another source of pressure.

Who the tools are for

They are designed for neurodivergent young adults and anyone who relates to planning through fluctuating energy, sensory overwhelm, executive dysfunction, burnout or the need for more predictable structure.

How Fleur approaches the work

Fleur combines lived experience, careful observation and a strong preference for clarity over hype. The goal is not to promise perfect routines. The goal is to create tools that still feel usable on difficult days.

Want to see the tools in action?

You can start with PRISMA Day for free, read the planning guides, or get in touch if you want to ask something first.
